About Me

I'm a web developer and artist who explores the dynamic fusion of technology and artistic expression. My large-scale drawings, created through bilateral and sensory mark-making techniques, celebrate the grandeur of the space. I engage the senses, transcending sight and weaving immersive experiences that go beyond the visual. As a web developer, I turn the digital realm into the space, crafting captivating websites. My portfolio showcases how web development and art converge to create innovative, compelling narratives. Join me on this journey at the intersection of technology and art as one. Collaboration, feedback, and exciting projects are always welcome as we continue to push the boundaries of creativity and innovation.
